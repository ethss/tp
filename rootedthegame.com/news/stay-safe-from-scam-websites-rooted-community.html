Stay Safe from Scam Websites

Be alert to scam sites impersonating Rooted's official website.

All the news > Stay Safe from Scam Websites

Published on Mar 03, 2024

We have recently identified attempts by fraudulent websites trying to impersonate Rooted's official platform. As we continue to ensure the safety and security of our community, we want to remind everyone that the only trusted source for information, updates, and downloads related to Rooted is through our official website. Please exercise caution and verify the authenticity of the website before sharing any personal information or downloading content. Stay vigilant and join us in safeguarding our community.

If you're ever in doubt about the legitimacy of an offer for Rooted, the safest course of action is to visit the game's Steam page and click on the "Visit the website" link. This ensures you're accessing the game through official and secure channels.

Please be aware that the only secure method to download Rooted is through Steam, using keys officially distributed by our team. Downloads from any other sources or accepting files from individuals can compromise your security and gaming experience. Always ensure your downloads are authenticated directly through Steam to guarantee your safety and the best gaming experience.

For more information and updates, always turn to our official website here, official X account, and official Discord server . 

Stay safe, survivors!
